Shattered Realms Races

Join our discord and lore discord for more information about the races, lore, and boons

HUMAN

Fortified, Tireless, Toughness   

Humans form tribes, clans, and civilizations. They gain a natural advantage to many of their characteristics. Based on Robert E. Howard's lore.

SUN ELF 

Toughness, Potent Sorcery, Homeostasis

Imperial and orderly variation of elves. From gleaming, golden cities they pursue magical research, endlessly seeking to gain a deeper understanding of the unseen forces that guide the weave.

MOON ELF

Night Vision, Hawkeye, Fight or Flight 

Curious, adventurous, and kind hearted race of humanoids hailing from the Fae Realm. Unlike their purist cousins the Sun Elves, they believe that interacting with other races is beneficial to all.

WOOD ELF

Fortified, Hawkeye, Tenacity

Much preferring the tall Oaks of their forest as opposed to the tall stone walls of many great Elven Cities. They do not hold any ill will towards their city dwelling cousins, and whenever they encounter other races they do their best to show them the wonders of the woods.

DARK ELF 

Night Vision, Potent Sorcery, Homeostasis

Harbors ancient loathing against Sun Elves

CAMBION

Night Vision, Troll Blood, Incorruptible

The unfortunate spawn of humans and demonic creatures, possess a natural aptitude for the arcane, and an innate pull towards dark pursuits.

NYMPHS

Water: Night Vision, Impervious, Amphibian

Sky: Flight, Fight or Flight, Impervious

Earth: Incorruptible, Impervious, Toughness

Wild, soothing, whimsical, yet oddly orderly. The elemental spirits of nature that manifest in areas dense with ancient magic, are walking contradictions sometimes, just as ferocious and destructive, calming and constructive as the natural forces they draw from.

DRAGONBORN

Cold Blooded, Troll Blood, Incorruptible

Large and burly, naturally aggressive.

WEREWOLVES

Shapeshifting

Wolfmen, beasts, lycans, werewolves. There are many names across the cultures to describe the beasts that lurk within the forests and even towns, hidden by a veil of an elven or a human face.

FELINIDAE

Night Vision, Fight or Flight, Tireless

Cat-like reflexes

KHORGROHK

Troll Blood, Tenacity, Toughness

Savage and brutal, the Khorgrohk are infamous for their warlike ways. The cities and villages that have suffered raids decades past are still spoken of in fearful whispers as if they were only recent. The Khorgrohk stand at an imposing height, with fearsome horns jutting from their thick skulls

KITSUNE

Night Vision, Shapeshifting, Potent Sorcery

In their natural form, they are anthropomorphic foxes. Despite their good intentions, kitsune were viewed negativity due to their trickster nature.

EARTH NYMPH

Incorruptible, Impervious, Toughness

Sneaks while crouching, shadowstep behind foes, and performs a back attack. Gains swiftness when in light and medium armor and increases damage and attack speed with agility.

DEEP ONE

Night Vision, Amphibian, Frostborn

Is a master of shapeshifting. Uses auras to restore health to themself and allies. Gains swiftness when in light and medium armor and cannot equip heavy armor. 

VAMPIRE

Night Vision, Incorruptible, Feast

A vampire exclusive class. Stealthy and powerful with magic. Gains swiftness with light and medium armor, utilizing some combat tactics.
