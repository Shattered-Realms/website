Shattered Realms Classes

WARRIOR

Vitality Strength Grit   

Specializes in two-handed weapons. Uses special combat attacks in melee. Regenerates health in combat when wearing medium or light armor.   

CLERIC 

Survival Vitality Encumbrance

A spellcaster who can use sorcery to heal allies in combat and utilize auras that cleanse corruption, heal, and restore stamina.  Specializes in one-handed weapons and shield. 

DEMONOLOGIST

Survival Encumbrance Vitality

Uses some spells, bonds with pets, shapeshifts into demon forms, and utilizes harmful auras against enemies. They cannot equip heavy armor. 

PALADIN 

Vitality Strength Encumbrance

A spellcaster who can cast a heal and utilize auras to buff allies. Specializes in two-handed weapons and uses some melee combat tactics.  

MAGE

Survival Accuracy Grit

Is a master of all things magic. Utilizes many spells in combat and harmful auras against enemies. They cannot wear heavy armor.

RANGER

Accuracy Vitality Survival

Bonds with a pet, gains attack speed and damage from agility. Gains critical strike with bows and swiftness in light and medium armor.

ROGUE 

Agility Grit Accuracy

Sneaks while crouching, shadowstep behind foes, and performs a back attack. Gains swiftness when in light and medium armor and increases damage and attack speed with agility.

DRUID 

Survival Agility Accuracy

Is a master of shapeshifting. Uses auras to restore health to themself and allies. Gains swiftness when in light and medium armor and cannot equip heavy armor. 

LORD OF NIGHT 

Vitality Grit Agility

A vampire exclusive class. Stealthy and powerful with magic. Gains swiftness with light and medium armor, utilizing some combat tactics.
