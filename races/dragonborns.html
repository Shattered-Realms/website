Dragonborns

Racial Information

  • Classes Applicable: Any
  • RP Modifiers: +2 Constitution, +2 Strength, +2 Charisma
  • Attribute Bonuses: +7 Vitality, +3 Grit +4 Strength +4 Survival. -5 Agility
  • Lifespan: 400 Years
  • Size: 1.1-1.2
  • Type: Beastkin

    • Mechanical Merits

  • Cold Blooded: the character is naturally colder and does better in warmer climates
  • Troll Blood: grants 2 healing every second
  • Incorruptible: immune to corruption

    • Boons

  • Tenacious Fortitude: Recovery from injury is 8 hours instead of 12
  • Iron Resolve: This character is immune to fear, or any ability/spell that would inflict that effect

    • Flaws

  • Warrior's Pride: This character may never flee from a PVP intent, nor may they ever surrender unless defeated.
  • Imposing Bulk: This character is too large and burly for small weaponry, and may not use daggers
