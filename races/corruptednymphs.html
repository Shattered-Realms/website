Corrupted Nymphs

Acquired Races Page

“Despite their foul hearts and evil spirits, these creatures possess the almost supernatural beauty of their more beneficent relatives. They resemble slim, full-bodied women, with delicate features. Thick, resplendent hair, full, pouty lips, and smooth-as-silk skin accentuate their triumphant beauty. Only a brief, calculating smile or an occasional hard glint from their piercing eyes betrays the evil nature of these creatures. Few people, however, can see beyond the unearthly beauty of a corrupted nymph, and into the blackened heart of the evil creature.“

When a nymph's nature is twisted and turned on its head, forced to abandon its nurturing side entirely, the result is often a twisted and devious creature that sows misery and malcontent wherever it goes. Having long since abandoned their duty to life and beauty, they relish in spreading corruption and deceit. Surrounded by an air of decay and suffering, Corrupted nymphs are harbingers of woe and death. Though they may hide their true nature from the unaware, those with second sight may be able to peer past their veneer of grace and beauty to see the withered, blackened heart beneath.

On the surface, they are beautiful, lithe, and graceful, appearing as ethereal and beguiling humanoids, though in their true form, the aspects of rot, decay, and death are plainly visible. Where an Earth nymph may be appearing as an avatar of the woodlands or plains, hair blossoming with growths and life, a corrupted nymph will appear as a withering mass of dead roots, decayed leaves, and dirt, or just a plain lifeless gray. Their eyes are black, bottomless pits, and gazing into them for too long may enthrall the weak-willed, or drive those of lesser faith into insanity.

Like regular nymphs, Corrupted nymphs are not commonplace in settled regions, though they are not shy of slithering around villages to spread their vile influence, or tempt mortals into straying from the path of life and light. Whereas earth nymphs and sky nymphs are seen as near-divine by those who come into contact with them, corrupted nymphs are the antithesis to all life, and seek only to spread rot, death, and misery in their wake.

There is no such thing as a naturally made corrupted nymph, as the name implies, they are always turned from their original path by an outside influence. Whether this be the blackening pull of supernatural forces and dark sorcery, or a horrific incident. They are quite cunning and predatory, though they may tempt mortals with bargains of power, desire, or material gain, their ultimate goal will always be to usurp and twist life away from its self-propagating cycle.

Requirements:

  • Pre-Requirements: Character must be a nymph of either variety in order to become a corrupted nymph.
  • Transformation: Through contact with corrupted places steeped in ancient sorcery, DM events, or horrific trauma twisting the nymph.

    • Racial Information

  • Classes Applicable: Mage, Druid, Demonologist
  • RP Modifiers: +2 Charisma, +2 Dexterity, +2 Wisdom
  • Attribute Bonuses: +7 Accuracy, +3 Vitality, +4 Strength +4 Agility
  • Lifespan: Ageless
  • Size: 0.85-0.95
  • Type: Fey

    • Mechanical Merits

  • Night Vision: able to see in the dark
  • Incorruptible: immune to corruption
  • Tenacity: gain HP and armor at low health
  • Impervious: immune to temperature effects

    • Boons

  • Darkness Calls: Gain advantage on all rolls when nighttime is active. (This will always be active in the Isle of Dusk)

  • Enthralling Gaze: Twist mortals into doing your bidding for one real-life hour, this is limited to commands that do not go over against that character’s beliefs or regular habits, and may not cause the target to inflict harm upon themselves. However, they will be unable to attack the caster and will consider the caster as beneficial and worthy of their protection.

    • Roll: caster's Negotiation vs target's Willpower

    Cooldown: Once per day

  • Shroud: On a successful roll, negate injury or mortal blows so long as the caster is not exposed to daylight or on a holy site

    • Roll: caster's Constitution vs target's Strength

    Cooldown: Once per day

    Flaws

  • Self Preservation: when outnumbered or at disadvantage, always flees from a hostile engagement.
  • Aversion to Sunlight: This creature becomes vulnerable to suffer final death when the sun is out
