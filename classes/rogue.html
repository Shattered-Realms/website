Rogue

Stats

  • Primary Stat: Agility
  • Secondary Stat: Grit
  • Minor Stat: Accuracy

    • Mechanical Class Abilities

  • Weapon Finesse: x% damage per agility. x% per str x% Attack Speed per agil (Attack Speed bonus negated by shield).

  • Tactics: Gives the player the ability to learn fighter skills and abilities.

  • Spellcaster: Gives the player the ability to cast Spells. Casting in medium armor imposes a 10% cast speed penalty. Casting in heavy armor imposes a 100% cast speed penalty.

  • Swiftness: Movement speed increased based on armor weight (6% Light / 5% Medium / 0% Heavy).

  • Stealth: Allows the player to crouch to enter stealth. The player will become undetectable by all non player-owned NPCs. When attacking from stealth, the player receives a +50% damage bonus on the stealth attack, this will be labelled as a Sneak Attack. Stealth will not work on NPCs that have already detected the player.

    • Roleplay Class Abilities and Spells

  • Passive - Fleet-Footed: Gain +1 to Dexterity +1 to Stealth

  • Shadow Stalk: The Rogue knows how to blend into the environment they are in better than most others, making it harder for their victims to percieve them. The rogue gains Advantage on all stealth rolls for 1 hour.

    • Roll: N/A

    Type: Ability

    Cooldown: Once per day

  • Smoke Bomb: The rogue grabs a small explosive device from their body, and tosses it towards the ground. The explosion is harmless but creates a flash with a large cloud of smoke. The caster is not effected by the smoke bomb, allies are effected, may be casted at themselves. All players within 5 foundations of the targeted player must roll constitution vs the caster's dexterity. If used as response to a PVP intent, and the targets fail the constitution save, they must count down from 10 to 0, rather than 5, while all others that succeeded on their save will begin the fight at 5.

    • Roll: Caster's Dexterity vs Target's Constitution

    Type: Ability

    Cooldown: Once per day

  • Acrobatic Dodge: The rogue uses their superior athleticism to avoid an incoming blow or spell. Upon a successful roll, the Rogue avoids one spell or ability that was used on them.

    • Roll: Dexterity vs Dexterity

    Type: Ability

    Cooldown: Once per day
