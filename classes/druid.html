Druid

Stats

  • Primary Stat: Survival
  • Secondary Stat: Accuracy
  • Minor Stat: Agility

    • Mechanical Class Abilities

  • Bow Mastery: Increases critical strike and critical strike damage done by ranged weapons.

  • Shapeshifter: Allows for the use of shapeshifts

  • Swiftness: Movement speed increased based on armor weight (6% Light / 5% Medium / 0% Heavy).

  • Aura Mastery: Unlocks the ability to learn auras. Your learned auras will be made available through the wheel menu accessed by holding F (unless otherwise rebinded)
