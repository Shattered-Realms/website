Paladin

Holy warriors, usually ordained by clergy, and bound by an oath wich they are sworn to uphold. Paladins range from crusaders in a royal entourage swearing fealty to a Monarch, to that of wandering vigilantes keeping whatever forces may hinder their objective at bay

Stats

  • Primary Stat: Vitality
  • Secondary Stat: Grit
  • Minor Stat: Encumbrance

    • Mechanical Class Abilities

  • Spellcaster: Gives the player the ability to cast Spells. Casting in medium armor imposes a 10% cast speed penalty. Casting in heavy armor imposes a 100% cast speed penalty.

  • Tactics: Gives the player the ability to learn fighter skills

  • Aura Mastery: Unlocks the ability to learn auras. Your learned auras will be made available through the wheel menu accessed by holding F (unless otherwise rebinded)

  • Armor Specialization: The player receives additional 40% damage reduction from armor. Additionally, the player has a 30% chance to avoid damage entirely based on the class of armor they are wearing (10% Light / 15% medium / 25% Heavy).

  • Infantry Training: Attacks with one handed weapons now scale with agility and/or strength. This feat grants +1% damage and +0.5% attack speed per Agility, and +1% damage per Strength. Using a shield reduces this bonus by half. When blocking with a shield or an equivalent defensive off-hand (such as the parrying dagger), you are given the buff called “Timed Block” if you block an attack within melee range during this short buff, you gain a buff called retaliation which can then be spent to perform a low stamina high damage light attack (LMB) or heavy attack (RMB). Both attacks give one stack of sunder in addition to whatever their normal effects are.

    • Roleplay Class Abilities and Spells

  • Passive - Divine Warden: You gain 1+ to your strength and charisma

  • Divine Sight: Peer past physical appearances, able to sense an opponents true nature. The target will feel the divine sight if used on them

    • Roll: Caster's Perception vs Target's Disguise

    Type: Clairvoyance

    Cooldown: N/A

  • Command - Halt!: Raise your voice with divine authority, ordering a target to halt in it's tracks. If cast as response to a PVP intent, and the target fails the intelligence save must count down to 10, rather than 5, while those who succeed, including the caster, may start moving after the regular 5. Those locked in a longer countdown may not be attacked.

    • Roll: Caster's Wisdom vs Target's Intelligence

    Type: Evocation Spell

    Cooldown: Once per day
