Demonologist

Stats

  • Primary Stat: Accuracy
  • Secondary Stat: Encumbrance
  • Minor Stat: Vitality

    • Mechanical Class Abilities

  • Spellcaster: Gives the player the ability to cast Spells. Casting in medium armor imposes a 10% cast speed penalty. Casting in heavy armor imposes a 100% cast speed penalty.

  • Shapeshifter: Allows for the use of shapeshifts

  • Beast Tamer: While following the owning player the pet gains the following bonuses: Your animal companion gains +3 to all attributes except accuracy. Additionally, your pet gains 50% of your Survival as a bonus as well. The animal companion buff provides a small heal over time effect to both the owning player as well as the pet. Both are healed for 2% of their max health every 1.5 seconds. Your pet also gains 10% damage reduction +0.4% per owning player's Survival.

  • Aura Mastery: Unlocks the ability to learn auras. Your learned auras will be made available through the wheel menu accessed by holding F (unless otherwise rebinded)
