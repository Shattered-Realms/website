Warrior

Stats

  • Primary Stat: Strength
  • Secondary Stat: Agility
  • Minor Stat: Grit

    • Mechanical Class Abilities

  • Barbarian: Gain up to 3 stacks of Battle Craze when struck by an enemy, while wearing medium or light armor regenerating 0.75% Total HP/Second per stack for 3 seconds. Max 3 Stacks

  • Two-handed Specialization: Two handed weapons receive an extra x% damage per point invested into Strength. Additionally, the player also gains up to 6 stacks of Furious Strikes each time an enemy is struck with a two handed weapon. The player receives +x strength and +x% attack speed per stack.

  • Infantry Training: Attacks with one handed weapons now scale with agility and/or strength. This feat grants +1% damage and +0.5% attack speed per Agility, and +1% damage per Strength. Using a shield reduces this bonus by half. When blocking with a shield or an equivalent defensive off-hand (such as the parrying dagger), you are given the buff called “Timed Block” if you block an attack within melee range during this short buff, you gain a buff called retaliation which can then be spent to perform a low stamina high damage light attack (LMB) or heavy attack (RMB). Both attacks give one stack of sunder in addition to whatever their normal effects are.

  • Leap Attack: While sprinting, perform a light attack (LMB) to make your character leap and do area of effect (AoE) damage to all the enemies in the area. Press heavy attack (RMB) during a sprint to perform a quick triple slash.
