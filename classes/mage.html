Mage

Stats

  • Primary Stat: Accuracy
  • Secondary Stat: Encumbrance
  • Minor Stat: Grit

    • Mechanical Class Abilities

  • Spellcaster: Gives the player the ability to cast Spells. Casting in medium armor imposes a 10% cast speed penalty. Casting in heavy armor imposes a 100% cast speed penalty.

  • Epic Toughness: The player receives +0.2% damage reduction per point of Survival. This damage reduction applies before damage reduced by armor alongside Bronzed Physique, IE: If you have 50 survival, are hit by an attack for 100, the damage from that attack will be reduced by 10% from the Epic Toughness trait, as well as an additional 20% If you have Bronzed Physique, and then the remaining amount (70) will finally be reduced by any armor you be wearing.

  • Flight: by holding down F mages can use their magic to propel themselves through the air.

  • Weapon Finesse: x% damage per agility. x% per str x% Attack Speed per agil (Attack Speed bonus negated by shield).
